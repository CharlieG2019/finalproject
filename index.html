New York Story

By Charlie Gomez

This collage I created is about me. Basically this collage shows my lifestyle. I was born and raised in Brooklyn, New York. Also I added music symbols because everywhere I go I listen to music and I love money too. I also got a picture of a train because I take the train to go to places. Overall, I am proud of being born and raised in Brooklyn.
